# Planning and Goals
## Core Values
- **Deep Work**
	- I believe deep work is the ability to focus without distraction on cognitively demanding tasks that create value and develop expertise.
	- I believe by engaging in deep work, while avoiding shallow & fragmented activities, I can live a more meaningful, productive and secure life.
- **Embodied Strength**
	- I believe in building physical and mental resilience through disciplined training and healthy habits.
	- I believe if I preserve my health I won't have to miss out on key life experiences and I can be there for my family and loved ones.
- **Relational Investment**
	- I believe in prioritizing and nurturing the people who matter most through consistent care, support, and quality time.
	- I believe meaningful relationships require intentional effort and that investing in others enriches both their lives and mine.
