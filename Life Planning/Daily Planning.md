# Process - Short Hand
## Start Routine
- 1. Review; Calendar, bullet + weekly goal.
- 2. Update; Weekly tracker.
- 3. Tasks; 1 MIT work + personal tasks.
- 4. Block; Kindly time block calendar.
- 5. Breathwork; min 1 minute.
## Finish Routine
- 1. Review; Calendar & bullet.
- 2. Reflection; Could the day have gone better?
- 3. Update; Calendar & bullet.
- 4. Meditation; 1 min +  
# Process - In Depth
- 1. Get out your Google Calendar, Bullet Journal and your Weekly Plan
	- Google Calendar - Time Blocking
	- Bullet Journal - task tracking / todo through the day.
	- Weekly plan - goals and focus for the week
- 2. Create a new entry in your bullet journal with today's date.
- 3. Review the previous day
	- Do you have any uncompleted tasks in your bullet journal?
		- Can they be done in a couple of mins?
			- Do them now
		- Otherwise schedule them as a future **Task** in Google calendar.
		- Or move them to today in the **Bullet Journal**.
		- Or put them in your [[Backlog]] note.
			- This is more for things that don't fit in your quarterly goals but would like a reminder to look at some day.
	- Did you missing anything from your calendar?
		- Reschedule or...
		- Move to [[Backlog]] note.
- 4. Prepare for the upcoming day
	- Review your Weekly Plan
		- Based on your weekly plan, time block your day.
			- For example, make sure you have time set aside for your fitness, health or study goals.
			- Make sure you put in breaks from work
			- And large uninterrupted chunks of work.
	- Update your bullet journal
		- Put all your tasks and personal appointments in your bullet journal
			- Don't need to put all of your work meetings in there, just bloat and they re-occur anyway.
