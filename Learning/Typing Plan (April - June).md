## ЁЯЧУя╕П Month 1: Lock In Your Foundation

**Weeks 1тАУ4 | Target: 55тАУ65 WPM**

### The Problem to Solve

You can touch type тАФ but inconsistently. Speed gains will stall unless every finger reliably hits its correct key every time. This month, slow down to speed up.

### Core Rule for Month 1

> **Accuracy over speed.** If you're making errors, slow down. Muscle memory built wrong is harder to fix than building it right from the start.

### Weekly Schedule

**Monday / Wednesday / Friday тАФ Foundation Drills (25 min)**

- 5 min warm-up: home row keys only on **TypingClub** or **Keybr**
- 15 min: **Keybr** finger-specific practice (focus on your weakest keys тАФ it will identify these automatically)
- 5 min: Note down any letters/combinations that felt slow or awkward

**Tuesday / Thursday тАФ Accuracy Practice (20 min)**

- 5 min warm-up on **MonkeyType** (punctuation off, short mode)
- 15 min: **TypeRacer** тАФ aim for >95% accuracy, not high WPM

**Saturday тАФ Weakness Work (20 min)**

- Compile your "struggle list" from the week (specific letters, bigrams like "qu", "th", "ion")
- Paste them into **MonkeyType тЖТ Custom** mode and drill repeatedly
- Run one 5-minute test at the end and log your WPM + accuracy

**Sunday тАФ Rest or Light Play**

- Optional: A few casual TypeRacer races for fun, no pressure

## ЁЯЧУя╕П Month 2: Build Speed

**Weeks 5тАУ8 | Target: 70тАУ80 WPM**

### The Problem to Solve

Foundation is solid. Now you need to push through the plateau by adding deliberate speed bursts and reading ahead while typing.

### Core Rule for Month 2

> **Practice reading 1тАУ2 words ahead** of where your fingers are. This is what separates fast typists from average ones.

### Weekly Schedule

**Monday / Wednesday тАФ Speed Drills (25 min)**

- 5 min: **10FastFingers** тАФ pure speed, don't worry about errors here
- 15 min: **MonkeyType** (quote mode, longest setting) тАФ push 5тАУ10 WPM above your comfort zone
- 5 min: Record your fastest burst and your average

**Tuesday / Thursday тАФ Accuracy & Real-World (25 min)**

- 5 min: **TypeRacer** warm-up (3 short races)
- 15 min: **MonkeyType** (English 1000 word list) тАФ aim for balance of speed and 95%+ accuracy
- 5 min: Practice the "ctrl+backspace" habit тАФ delete whole words, not letters

**Friday тАФ Weakness Drill (20 min)**

- Review your week's missed words
- **MonkeyType тЖТ Practice Missed Words** (after any session, this option appears)
- Custom drill: any bigrams/trigrams still slowing you down

**Saturday тАФ Speed Test & Log (15 min)**

- Take a 1-minute and a 5-minute test on MonkeyType
- Log both тАФ the 5-minute test is your real score
- Celebrate any milestone reached! ЁЯОЙ

## ЁЯТб Key Rules to Remember
1. **Accuracy first, speed follows.** Never sacrifice accuracy for speed during practice.
2. **Slow down to speed up.** If you're making lots of errors, drop 10 WPM and rebuild clean habits.
3. **Use ctrl+backspace** to delete whole words тАФ it's faster and trains good habits.
4. **Drill your weak keys.** Keybr identifies them automatically тАФ trust the process.
5. **Plateaus are normal.** You may feel stuck for 1тАУ2 weeks тАФ then you'll suddenly jump. Keep going.
6. **5-minute tests = your real WPM.** Short tests flatter you. Use 5-minute tests as your honest benchmark.
7. **Daily > occasional.** 20 minutes every day beats 2 hours once a week.
