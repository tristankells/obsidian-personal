# Overview
- [Youth Mobility Scheme visa: Overview - GOV.UK](https://www.gov.uk/youth-mobility)
## How long it takes
- The earliest you can apply for a visa is 6 months before you travel
	- *Example: You can apply from 16 January if you plan to travel on 15 July.*
- Once you’ve applied online, proved your identity and provided your documents, you’ll usually get a decision on your visa within **3 weeks**.
# Eligibility
- [Youth Mobility Scheme visa: Eligibility - GOV.UK](https://www.gov.uk/youth-mobility/eligibility)
- You can apply for the visa if you’re aged **18** to **35** and from:
	- Australia
	- Canada
	- **New Zealand**
- You must be 35 or under when you apply for it, but you can be over 35 when it starts.
## Money to support yourself
- You must have at least £2,530 (**$5,886.30** NZD) in your bank account to show you can support yourself in the UK.
- You will need to have had the money available for at least 28 days in a row. Day 28 must be within 31 days of applying for this visa. *TLDR; 62 days*
## Documents you must provide
- [Youth Mobility Scheme visa: Documents you must provide - GOV.UK](https://www.gov.uk/youth-mobility/documents-you-must-provide)
- When you apply you’ll need to provide:
	- A valid passport or other document that shows your identity and nationality.
	- A bank statement showing [you have at least £2,530 in savings](https://www.gov.uk/youth-mobility/eligibility).
	- Your [tuberculosis (TB) test results](https://www.gov.uk/tb-test-visa) if you’re from a country where you have to take the test.
		- You **DON'T** need this in New Zealand.
- You must visit a visa application center to complete the application process:
	- Huawei Centre, Level 8, 120 Albert Street, Auckland CBD, Auckland 1010, New Zealand
	- [Auckland | vfsglobal](https://visa.vfsglobal.com/nzl/en/gbr/attend-centre/auckland)
	- Follow the supporting document guide here to avoid unexpected fees: https://visa.vfsglobal.com/nzl/en/gbr/submitting-supporting-documents
## Additional Costs
- Immigration Health Surcharge (IHS)
	- £776 (NZD $1,805.44) per year for students, their dependents, and **those on a Youth Mobility Scheme visa**, for example £1,552 (NZD $3,610.88) for a 2-year visa
		- Example: £2,075 for a 2-year visa (**$4,816.06**)
- Application fee
	- £319 (NZD $742.19)
## Extend your visa
- You can only apply to extend your Youth Mobility Scheme visa if you’re from Australia, Canada or New Zealand.
- You can extend your visa for one year.
- To extend your visa, you must:
	- be living in the UK
	- have a valid Youth Mobility Scheme visa
## Fees
You’ll need to pay:
- the £319 application fee
- the £776 immigration health surcharge
