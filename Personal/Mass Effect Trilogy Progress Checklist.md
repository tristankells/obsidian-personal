⚠️ **After IFF, do NOT do N7 missions or DLC until post-game**

- [ ] **Legion: Loyalty** | _Tali_ - Not much more dialogue but fitting
- [ ] **Obtain 300,000 total minerals** - Gives war asset in ME3

### Suicide Mission

- [ ] **Suicide Mission Prep:**

### Romance Lock-In Note

At this point, initiate romance by talking to squadmates. Talk to desired romance first or reject each directly. If romancing Tali, wait until after Geth Dreadnought. If romancing Miranda, wait until 3rd encounter.

### Mid-Game Missions

- [ ] **Citadel: Medical Supplies**
- [ ] **Citadel: Volus Ambassador**
- [ ] **Omega DLC** - Fits best here (downtime in story, retaliation against Cerberus)

### Rannoch Arc

- [ ] **Priority: Perseus Veil**
- [ ] **Priority: Geth Dreadnought** | _Garrus/James/EDI/ME1 LI_ - Lock Tali romance before this if romancing her. Bring ME1 LI if cheated with Tali. Bring Garrus if romancing him. James/EDI have funny dialogue otherwise
- [ ] **N7: Fuel Reactor**

⚠️ **Both Rannoch missions:** Expire after Priority: Rannoch with major consequences

- [ ] **Rannoch: Geth Fighter Squadrons** | _Tali & Anyone_ - **Major consequences if not done**
- [ ] **Priority: Rannoch** | _Tali & Anyone_ - Javik gets different post-mission dialogue. Bring favorite for important mission

### Thessia & Beyond

- [ ] **Priority: Thessia** | _Javik & Liara_ - Javik has most unique dialogue for single squadmate in game. Use LEFT dialogue at end to charm/intimidate TIM
- [ ] **N7: Communication Hub**
- [ ] **Priority: Horizon** | _Kaidan/Ashley & Anyone_ - Virmire survivor was here in ME2 (no special dialogue though)

### Leviathan DLC

**Note:** Awkward pacing no matter when done. Can spread 3 missions throughout game but feels awkward. If spreading: 1st after Omega, 2nd after Rannoch, 3rd after Thessia.

- [ ] **Leviathan: Mahavid** | _EDI & Anyone_ - EDI has interesting things to say
- [ ] **Leviathan: Namakli**
- [ ] **Leviathan: Despoina** | _Your LI & Anyone_ - LI has unique scene

### Citadel DLC

⚠️ **Do as VERY LAST THING before point of no return**

- [ ] **Silver Coast Casino: Infiltration** | _Your LI_ - Lots of dialogue for every character. LI or Wrex recommended
- [ ] **Citadel Archives** | _Wrex & Your LI_ - LI has unique dialogue
- [ ] **Citadel Docks: Retake Normandy** - EDI has unique shuttle scene. Garrus has dialogue if brought to Mordin's loyalty in ME2. Every squadmate combo has interactions
- [ ] **Citadel Aftermath** - Explore Silversun Strip. Meet characters via emails (exit/reenter apartment for more). Joker/Wrex at casino bar. Kasumi at suspicious device on casino ground floor. Jack emails after Armax Arsenal Arena

### Endgame

- [ ] **Priority: Cerberus Headquarters** | _Your LI_ - LI has dialogue viewing video logs. Choose LEFT dialogue to charm/intimidate TIM
- [ ] **Priority: Earth - 1st Half** | _James/Kaidan/Ashley & Anyone_ - One reacts to something (only one can speak)
- [ ] **Priority: Earth - Middle Section** - Room behind Kaidan/Ashley lets you say goodbye to ME2 squadmates & Cortez
- [ ] **Priority: Earth - 2nd Half** | _Your LI & Anyone_ - LI gets special scene if present. Different scene with squadmates if LI not present
