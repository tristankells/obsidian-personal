## Critical Statistics & Racial Dynamics

In _Baldur's Gate 3_, the Swords Bard is statistically the most popular choice for "Honor Mode" completions due to its versatility.
- **Racial Performance:** **Lightfoot Halflings** are highly ranked for this build because **Halfling Luck** allows you to reroll "1s" on attack rolls. When using _Sharpshooter_ (which gives a −5 penalty to hit), avoiding critical failures is mathematically significant, reducing your miss chance by approximately **5%**.
- **Skill Mastery:** Bards receive **Jack of All Trades**, adding half their proficiency bonus to all non-proficient checks. At Level 12, this is a **+2** bonus to every single skill, making them the ultimate "Face" of the party.
- **Burst Potential:** With **Action Surge** and **Slashing Flourish**, you can fire **8 arrows** in a single turn. With **Titanstring Bow** and **27 Strength** (via Elixir), your flat damage bonus per turn can exceed **+120** before rolling any dice.

## Essential Endgame Gear
- **Head:** **Helmet of Arcane Acuity** (Act 2, Reithwin Mason's Guild). This is the build's engine.
- **Ring:** **Band of the Mystic Scoundrel** (Act 3, Circus). Allows you to cast Illusion/Enchantment spells as a **Bonus Action** after hitting a weapon attack.
- **Weapon:** **Titanstring Bow** (Act 1). Scaled with **Gauntlets of Frost Giant Strength** (Act 3).
- **Armor:** **Armor of Agility** (Act 3). Adds full Dex modifier to AC; you will likely have **22+ AC** while wearing "Medium" armor.

---
## The "God-Tier" Turn 1 Rotation
1. **Action:** Use **Slashing Flourish (Ranged)** twice (4 hits).
2. **Action Surge:** Use **Slashing Flourish (Ranged)** twice more (4 hits).
    - _Result: 8 hits = +10 Max Stacks of Arcane Acuity._
3. **Bonus Action (via Ring):** Cast **Hold Monster** or **Confusion**.
    - _Result: Because of your +10 stacks, the enemy usually has a 0% to 5% chance to save. They are effectively removed from the game._

# Gloomstalker Assassin (Astarion) https://gamestegy.com/post/bg3/864/assassin-rogue-build-stalker

## Build Core
- **Final Class Split:** 5 Ranger (Gloomstalker) / 4 Rogue (Assassin) / 2 Fighter / 1 Cleric (War Domain)
- **Primary Stat:** Dexterity (20+)
- **Combat Loop:** Start from stealth → Proc **Surprise** → Use **Action Surge** → Delete enemies with guaranteed Crits.

## Essential Gear & Consumables

### The "Must-Haves"
- **Ranged Setup:** **Titanstring Bow** (Act 1) + **Club of Hill Giant Strength** (Off-hand). This adds your Strength modifier to every bow shot.
- **Critical Fishing:** **Knife of the Undermountain King** (Act 1) reduces the roll needed for a crit.
- **Armor:** **The Graceful Cloth** (Act 1) for +2 Dex or **Spidersilk Armour** for Stealth Advantage.
- **Cloak:** **Deathstalker Mantle** (Dark Urge only) for Invisibility after every kill.
### Consumables
- **Elixir of Bloodlust:** Gain an extra action on a kill (Best for multi-target).
- **Elixir of Cloud Giant Strength:** If using Titanstring Bow, this sets STR to 27 for massive damage.
- **Arrow of Many Targets:** Allows your massive single-target damage to chain to 3 additional enemies.

## Combat Strategy: 6+ Attacks per Turn
To achieve the maximum burst on Turn 1:
1. **Attack from Stealth:** This triggers a Surprise round.
2. **Dread Ambusher:** Use your free Gloomstalker attack.
3. **Main Actions:** Use 2 attacks (Extra Attack).
4. **Action Surge:** Use 2 more attacks.
5. **War Priest:** Use your Bonus Action for a 6th attack.
6. **Assassin Reset:** Because you are an Assassin, your Action/Bonus Action points refresh the moment formal initiative starts, potentially extending the chain.
