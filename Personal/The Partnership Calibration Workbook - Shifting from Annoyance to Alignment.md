## Part III: Building a New Communication System (The Strategy)

To meet both your need for focus and her need for support, you need a shared vocabulary for requests.

### **A. Your Wife's New Request Rule (The "Yellow Light" Rule)**

- **Goal:** To give you a buffer and remove the element of surprise.
    
- **Wife agrees to use these phrases (or similar):**
    
    - "When you're at a good stopping point, could you help me with X?"
        
    - "Do you have a few minutes for a quick favor, or should I catch you later?"
        
    - "I need your eyes on something, but it's not urgent. When's a good time?"
        

### **B. Your New Response Scripts (The "Pause & Respond" Tool)**

- **Goal:** To communicate a "No" or a "Wait" kindly, preventing the annoyance from showing.
    
- **The Three Scripts to Master:**
    

|If the request is...|Script to Use (Kind and Firm)|What it achieves|
|---|---|---|
|**Immediate Interrupting**|**"I want to help you, but I'm losing my thought right now. Give me ten minutes to wrap this up, and I will come find you."**|Respects your focus; promises future help (she feels secure).|
|**A Big, Grudging Ask**|**"I can tackle [Specific Small Part] right now, but I can't take on all of X. Let's look at the whole thing tonight and decide how to split it."**|Prevents full resentment; establishes teamwork/boundaries.|
|**Easily Solved Alone**|**"I'm happy to, but first, did you already try [One simple suggestion]? If that doesn't work, I'm on my way."**|Gently promotes her competence while still being available.|
## Part IV: The Relationship Review (The Check-In)

Schedule a short, low-pressure conversation with your wife to review how the new system is working.

- **Date of Check-In:**
    
- **My Successes This Week:** (List times you successfully used a script or paused before reacting.)
    
- **Her Feedback to Me:** (Ask her: "Did I seem more present when I helped this week? Was there a time I successfully said 'Not now' that felt okay?")
    
- **My Feedback to Her:** (Tell her: "I really appreciated you using the 'Yellow Light' phrase on Tuesday. It helped me shift gears better.")
    
- **Next Action Step:** What is one small, specific thing we will both work on next week? (E.g., "I will ask for her help on one small task." or "She will only use the yellow light rule.")
