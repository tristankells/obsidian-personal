# Best Tempest Cleric Build - Baldur's Gate 3 (BG3) https://gamestegy.com/post/bg3/871/tempest-domain-cleric-build

## Build Core
- **Final Class Split:** 11 Tempest Cleric / 1 Storm Sorcerer.
- **The "Storm Nova" Mechanic:** The combination of the **Wet** status (doubles Lightning/Cold damage) and **Destructive Wrath** (forces a spell to deal maximum damage instead of rolling) allows you to hit for guaranteed, doubled maximum damage.
- **Crowd Control:** The **Thunderbolt Strike** feature allows you to knock enemies backward whenever you deal Lightning or Thunder damage, effectively keeping threats away from your backline.

## Statistics & Racial Effectiveness
The Tempest Cleric is a "WIS-DEX" hybrid build, prioritizing spell success and initiative.
- **Racial Impact:** * **Wood Half-Elf:** The **1.5m movement bonus** is statistically the best choice. Since **Spirit Guardians** requires you to move near enemies to damage them, higher speed equals more targets hit per turn.
    - **High Elf/Half-Elf:** Provides a free **Intelligence-based Cantrip**. Choose _Minor Illusion_ to group enemies together before casting a maximized _Shatter_ or _Call Lightning_.
- **The Math of Destructive Wrath:** A Level 3 _Call Lightning_ normally deals 3d10 damage (average 16.5). With **Destructive Wrath** and the **Wet** condition, it deals a flat **60 damage** (30 maxed × 2 for Wet). This removes the "RNG" factor from your most important turns.

## Essential Equipment
- **Luminous Armour (Act 1):** Every time you deal Radiant damage (from _Spirit Guardians_), you apply **Radiating Orb**, giving enemies a -1 penalty to their Attack Rolls.
- **Gloves of Belligerent Skies (Act 1):** Dealing Thunder, Lightning, or Radiant damage applies **Reverberation**, further penalizing enemy physical saves and potentially knocking them prone.
- **Markoheshkir (Act 3):** The legendary staff. Use the **Bolts of Doom** attunement to gain _Chain Lightning_, which—when maximized by your Cleric features—can delete entire groups of enemies instantly.

---
## Combat Strategy: "The Perfect Storm"
1. **Preparation:** Cast **Create Water** (or throw a Water Bottle) to apply the **Wet** status to as many enemies as possible.
2. **The Burst:** Cast **Call Lightning** or **Destructive Wave**. Use your **Channel Divinity: Destructive Wrath** to ensure every bolt hits for maximum damage.
3. **The Defense:** If an enemy gets close, your **Wrath of the Storm** reaction strikes them back. If they try to hit you, your **Shield** spell (from the Sorcerer dip) adds +5 to your AC.
4. **Utility:** Between storms, keep **Spirit Guardians** active and run through the battlefield to debuff every enemy with Radiating Orbs.

# Abjuration Wizard build - Ultimate Tank (Gale)
| https://gamestegy.com/post/bg3/995/abjuration-wizard-build

## Build Core
- **Final Class Split:** 10 Abjuration Wizard / 1 Draconic Sorcerer / 1 Tempest Cleric.
- **The "Arcane Ward" Mechanic:** Every time you cast an Abjuration spell (like _Shield_ or _Counterspell_), you build stacks of Arcane Ward (max 20). This ward acts as a flat damage reduction. If you have 20 stacks and take 18 damage, you actually take **0 damage**.
- **The Reflective "Thorns" Combo:** By combining **Armour of Agathys** (from Sorcerer) and **Fire Shield: Chill** (from Wizard), enemies take massive Cold damage when they hit you. Because your Arcane Ward soaks the hit, your temporary HP from _Agathys_ stays active, allowing the "thorns" effect to last indefinitely.

## Statistics & Racial Effectiveness
In high-level _Baldur's Gate 3_ play (specifically Honor Mode), the Abjuration Wizard is statistically the most survivable caster.
- **Damage Mitigation Math:** With **Heavy Armour Master** and a Level 10 Arcane Ward, you can effectively ignore up to **23 points of incoming damage** per hit. Since the average Act 3 enemy hits for 15–25 damage, you become functionally immortal against standard mobs.
- **Spell Save DC:** With 20 Intelligence and endgame gear like the **Hood of the Weave**, your DC will reach **21+**. This ensures your _Counterspells_ and _Globe of Invulnerability_ almost never fail.

---
## The "Ultimate Tank" Rotation
1. **Before Combat:** Cast **Armour of Agathys** at your highest spell level. Use **Warding Bond** (via an ally or item) to grant yourself Resistance to all damage.
2. **Turn 1:** Cast **Fire Shield: Chill**. Use **Create Water** to make enemies **Wet** (this doubles your reflective Cold damage).
3. **The Strategy:** Walk directly past enemies to trigger their **Opportunity Attacks**.
    - The enemy hits you.
    - Your **Arcane Ward** reduces the damage to 0.
    - The enemy takes **40-60 Cold damage** (doubled to 80-120 if Wet) from _Agathys_ and _Fire Shield_.
    - You remain at full health while they die on your turn.
